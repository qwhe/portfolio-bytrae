About

Designer Profile

Based in Beijing, I am a multidisciplinary designer specializing in brand gift packaging, IP derivatives, and H5 interactive design. With years of experience collaborating with leading technology companies, I transform brand stories into tangible, memorable experiences through thoughtful packaging and product design.

My work spans from concept development to final production, covering the full spectrum of brand merchandise — from premium festival gift boxes to creative desk ornaments and gaming IP collectibles. Every project begins with understanding the brand's DNA and ends with a product that resonates emotionally with its audience.
